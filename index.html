Our Products

Our solutions enables Individuals, Businesses and Organizations to access verified Identity and address data to support their due diligence practice in order to safely transact business.

Address Validation and Verification

  • Address Validation - Easily confirm validity of addresses using our service to conduct in-person physical validation across Africa.

  • Instant Address Verification - Instantly verify addresses in minutes using the Comono i-Confirm database.

  • Address Autocomplete - Autocomplete addresses at the point - of - entry with correct data integration with your workflow using our autocomplete API.

Identity Verification

Instantly verify a wide range of recognized ID cards and know the true identity of your customers, partners, suppliers or new hires.

Project Verification

Use the Comono ‘Check-My-Project’ tool to remotely verify and monitor the status of your projects. Let Comono be the third eye that ensures your contractor’s compliance with the agreed project terms and deliverables, detect and prevent fraud before it happens.
